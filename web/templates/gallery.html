Exoplanet Palette

the colour scheme of every known exoplanet, derived from physics

Each planet's colour is computed from its reflected-light spectrum (albedo model × host-star spectrum) via CIE 1931 colour matching. Every planet is shown two ways: the full modelled spectrum, and what the Roman Coronagraph would recover from just four bands. Both are modelled predictions, not photographs. For Roman's ~dozen tech-demo targets, the four-band view will one day be replaced by real measured data. Planet and star data: NASA Exoplanet Archive.
